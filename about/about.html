About Too Much Tech

Discover the core mission and the dedicated individuals working to build a healthier digital world.

Why We Exist

In a world where we are constantly bombarded by notifications, emails, and infinite scroll feeds, our brains are working overtime. Too Much Tech (TMT) was born out of a simple realization: we are more connected than ever, yet we feel increasingly drained and isolated.

Our primary purpose is to bridge the gap between necessary technology use and mental well-being. We dive deep into the science of digital fatigue to provide you with actionable tools, educational resources, and community support to help you unplug when necessary.

We don't believe technology is evil—we just believe your brain deserves a break. Our goal is to empower users to reclaim their time and build a healthier, more intentional relationship with their devices.

The Origin Story

The foundation of Too Much Tech was laid during a time of unprecedented digital reliance. As students and young professionals navigating the sudden shift to remote work and entirely online learning environments, our team experienced firsthand the overwhelming effects of continuous screen exposure.

What started as casual conversations about "Zoom fatigue" and eye strain quickly evolved into a serious inquiry into our collective digital habits. We realized that our experiences were not isolated incidents, but symptoms of a much larger, modern epidemic.

Our Core Values

Intentional Balance

We advocate for using technology as a tool, not a crutch. We help you find the sweet spot between productivity and digital burnout.

Mindful Living

We believe in the power of being present. Our solutions encourage structured, screen-free moments to rest and reset naturally.

Empathetic Support

Digital fatigue affects everyone differently. We strive to create a judgment-free community with realistic, adaptable solutions.
