Who We Are

We are 2nd-year BSIT students from the University of the East - Manila who are developing this website to explore the effects of modern technology on human behavior and mental health. Our goal is to spread awareness about digital fatigue and encourage healthier technology habits. Our team collaborated to research information, organize website content, and design a website that is both educational and easy to understand.

Our purpose and goal is to help individuals and others navigate the digital world with educational resources.
