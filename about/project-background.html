Project Background

How Too Much Tech evolved from a shared frustration into an active movement.

The Origin Story

The foundation of Too Much Tech was laid during a time of unprecedented digital reliance. As students and young professionals navigating the sudden shift to remote work and entirely online learning environments, our team experienced firsthand the overwhelming effects of continuous screen exposure.

What started as casual conversations about "Zoom fatigue" and eye strain quickly evolved into a serious inquiry into our collective digital habits. We realized that our experiences were not isolated incidents, but symptoms of a much larger, modern epidemic.

From Research to Action

We began extensively researching the psychological and physical impacts of extended screen time, discovering that digital fatigue actively hinders productivity, disrupts sleep patterns, and elevates anxiety.

Armed with this data and a shared desire for change, we initiated this project to create a centralized resource for digital wellness. The goal was to build a platform that doesn't just present the problem—like endless doom-scrolling—but actively provides structured, evidence-based solutions.

Today, Too Much Tech serves as both an educational hub and an ongoing project. We continually update our findings, refine our web platform, and engage with our community to discover what truly helps people disconnect and recharge in a tech-saturated era.
