Why We Exist

In a world where we are constantly bombarded by notifications, emails, and infinite scroll feeds, our brains are working overtime. Too Much Tech (TMT) was born out of a simple realization: we are more connected than ever, yet we feel increasingly drained and isolated.

Our primary purpose is to bridge the gap between necessary technology use and mental well-being. We dive deep into the science of digital fatigue to provide you with actionable tools, educational resources, and community support to help you unplug when necessary.

We don't believe technology is evil—we just believe your brain deserves a break. Our goal is to empower users to reclaim their time and build a healthier, more intentional relationship with their devices.

Our Core Values

Intentional Balance

We advocate for using technology as a tool, not a crutch. We help you find the sweet spot between productivity and digital burnout, ensuring your tech serves you, not the other way around.

Mindful Living

We believe in the power of being present. Our solutions encourage structured, screen-free moments that allow your mind to rest, reset naturally, and engage deeply with the physical world.

Empathetic Support

Digital fatigue affects everyone differently. We strive to create a judgment-free community where people can openly share their struggles and discover realistic, adaptable solutions.

Too Much Tech

An educational platform raising awareness about digital fatigue. Explore our resources to understand the mental and physical impacts of excessive tech use, and learn how to build healthier digital habits today.
