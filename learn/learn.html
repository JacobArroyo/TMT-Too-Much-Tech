Learn more about Digital Fatigue

Have you ever found yourself staring at a screen for hours, reading the same sentence over and over while your eyes feel heavy and your focus begins to blur? That "foggy" sensation isn't just common tiredness—it is digital fatigue,

It happens when our "biological hardware"—our brains and eyes—struggles to process the infinite stream of data, blue light, and constant notifications we encounter every day.

By understanding the science behind why our screens drain us, we can move past the "infinite scroll" and start building a more intentional, balanced relationship with the technology we use.
