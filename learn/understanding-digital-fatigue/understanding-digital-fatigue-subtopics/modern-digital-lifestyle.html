Modern Digital Lifestyle

Author: Ralph Galapin Depollo

Source: https://www.freepik.com/vectors/digital-lifestyle

The modern digital lifestyle means that technology is in every part of our lives. We have smart homes, watches that track our heart, and apps for everything. While this is very convenient, it often means we are just following what a screen tells us to do instead of making our own choices.

This lifestyle has changed how we spend time with others. You might see a group of friends eating together, but everyone is looking at their own phone. This is called "phubbing." Even though people are physically together, they are actually alone in their own digital worlds.

Even though we are more "connected" than ever, many people feel more lonely. Online likes and comments are not the same as a real hug or a real conversation. As we spend more time on screens, we lose the small, unplanned moments with people that make us feel like we belong.

A digital lifestyle also ruins our sleep. The blue light from screens tells our brain that it is daytime, so it doesn't get ready for sleep. This makes us stay up late, which makes digital fatigue even worse the next day. A healthy life needs a balance between the screen and the real world.

Parts of a Digital Lifestyle:

  • Constant Gadgets: Using technology from the moment you wake up until you sleep.
  • Social Distancing: Being on your phone while around other people.
  • Low Quality Sleep: Trouble resting because of blue light from screens.
  • Digital Loneliness: Feeling alone even with many friends online.
