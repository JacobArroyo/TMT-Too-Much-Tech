Understanding Digital Fatigue

Author: Ralph Galapin Depollo

Source: https://fit-counseling.com/understanding-digital-fatigue-and-how-to-combat-it/

Digital fatigue happens when we use computers, phones, and tablets for too long. It makes our brains feel very tired and makes it hard to focus or remember things. This topic looks at how our modern life with technology changes the way we think and how our brains work.

Our brains are not used to looking at screens all day. When we talk to people online, our brains have to work much harder to understand them compared to talking in person. This extra work uses up a lot of mental energy, which leads to a "foggy" feeling in our heads where we can't think clearly.

By studying this, we can see how being "always online" and having too much information causes stress. We will explore why we become so dependent on gadgets, how we forget things because we can just "Google" them, and how our brains actually change their shape because of our digital habits.
