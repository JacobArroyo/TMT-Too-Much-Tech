Digital Detox

Author: Jazztine Kurt U. Ramos

Source: https://cemer.com/en/what-is-a-digital-detox

Too much screen time can sneak into our lives without us realizing. I’ve gone through sleepless nights, browsing through feeds, and sensed an increasing feeling of anxiety and detachment due to too much screen time. Digital exhaustion, that sense of mental weariness following prolonged screen time, can set in without warning. The endless flow of impeccably arranged content in my feed is hard to overlook, leading me to doubt whether I'm measuring up. And we shouldn't overlook the physical impact—my screen frequently traps me in my chair, limiting my physical movement.

A Digital Detox involves a duration without utilizing any digital gadgets. I believed I wouldn’t make it through one! I not only survived, but I also felt mentally rejuvenated afterward. I started with a basic promise: to be without my phone for two hours daily initially, then for a whole day! My initial digital detox was challenging; I would mindlessly grasp for my phone while working, but my efficiency increased significantly, which became the motivation itself.
