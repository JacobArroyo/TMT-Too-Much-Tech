Trend Pressure

Author: Darene Hanz H. Tunod

Source: https://socialpubli.com/blog/how-to-be-trending-topic-on-twitter/

Trend pressure is basically a "tug-of-war" in your head...

The Mental Cost of Keeping Up With Trends:

  • Fear of Exclusion: The deep worry that if you aren't watching what’s popular, you’ll lose your "seat at the table" in group chats.
  • The Performance Gap: Watching shows just so you can "perform" your knowledge in comments or conversations instead of actually enjoying the story.
  • Losing Yourself: Relying on the "Trending" tab makes you lose your own internal compass, which leaves you feeling empty because you aren't picking things you actually like.
  • The Digital Treadmill: Trying to keep up with trends that reset every 24 hours keeps your brain on "high alert" and stops you from ever truly relaxing.

The Fear of Exclusion is probably the biggest reason we feel stressed...

This leads to what is known as Performance Consumption...

When you let a Trending tab pick your schedule...

We often keep following these trends even when they’re burning us out...
