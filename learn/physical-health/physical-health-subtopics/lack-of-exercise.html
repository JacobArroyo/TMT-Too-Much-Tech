Lack of Exercise

Author: Jacob Rex A. Arroyo

The "sedentary lifestyle" means sitting down almost all day because of technology. When we sit for too long, our metabolism slows down. This means our bodies become worse at burning fat and controlling blood sugar, which is bad for our long-term health.

In the past, people had to move more for daily tasks. Now, we don't have to walk to the store or stand in line because we can do everything on an app. This lack of movement makes our muscles weaker and our joints feel stiff and painful.

Sitting for many hours is now considered very dangerous for the heart. Even if you go to the gym later, sitting all day at a desk still hurts your circulation. Your body needs regular movement to keep your blood flowing and your muscles active.

To stay healthy, try "movement snacks." Stand up and move around for five minutes every hour. Even a short walk around the room can help "reset" your body and keep your heart healthy while you work on your computer.

Risks of Not Moving:

  • Slow Metabolism: The body burns less energy when we sit all day.
  • Muscle Weakness: Muscles and joints becoming stiff from not being used.
  • Heart Health: Higher risks for heart problems due to sitting too much.
  • Movement Snacks: Taking short breaks to stand up and walk every hour.
