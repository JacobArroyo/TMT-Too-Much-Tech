Nature Breaks

Author: Jazztine Kurt U. Ramos

Source: https://hr.umich.edu/about-uhr/uhr-news/embrace-nature-mental-restoration

Nature breaks, preferably taken without screens, alleviate digital fatigue by inducing "soft fascination," a condition that enables the brain's attention systems to rejuvenate while lowering stress hormones such as cortisol. Being in green areas—like parks or woods—offers visual and sound relief, reducing heart rates and promoting mental rejuvenation.

How Nature Breaks Help With Digital Fatigue

  • Attention Restoration Theory (ART): Research shows that natural settings draw attention easily, enabling the brain to recuperate from the strong concentration (directed attention) demanded by screens.
  • Reduced Mental Fatigue: Being in nature, instead of engaging in online activities, rejuvenates mental ability and alleviates sensations of being overwhelmed.
  • Physiological Calm: Nature reduces blood pressure, heart rate, and cortisol levels, counteracting the "fight or flight" response frequently activated by digital, high-speed work settings.
  • Sensory Grounding: Nature offers sensory stimuli (aromas, surfaces, breeze) that anchors the individual in the tangible, current environment, aiding in transcending the "digital haze" of constant screen usage.
  • Creativity Boost: Taking a break in a green area provides a mental refresh, enhancing concentration and boosting creative ideas.

Effective Nature Break Strategies

  • Unplug Completely: To achieve optimal rejuvenation, refrain from using electronic devices while outdoors. A nature break alongside a laptop is essentially not a break whatsoever.
  • Short Breaks Matter: A 15-minute session of sitting or strolling in a green space can greatly lessen stress.
  • Seek “Soft Fascination”: Select settings that gently captivate you, like observing trees, water, or clouds, permitting your thoughts to wander and rejuvenate.
  • Daily Exposure: Include, if feasible, a brief period of outdoor time each day, like a stroll in a nearby park or a peaceful lunch break outdoors.
